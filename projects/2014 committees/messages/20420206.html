As a way to share our registration advertising efforts as well brainstorm other ways we can reach our target audience, here is a summary of our efforts:
  • posting on home page of website
  • email(s) sent to our internal list (multiple times)
  • Quarter-page ad in Milton Times (at least twice, first one runs 1/23)
  • Facebook posts
  • Twitter posts
  • Milton Patch - http://milton.patch.com/groups/announcements/p/milton-girls-softball-2014-spring-registration-is-open
  • 02186mytownmatters.com
  • PTOs - Facebook, website or e-mail blast
  • FaceBook Milton Neighbors
  • Collicot, Cunningham, Glover, Tucker & Pierce PTO's
  • Milton PS Central Admin for Backpack Notices

Targeted Direct Mail
We looked at targeted direct mail to specific households in Milton as a way to reach families who are unaware or unfamiliar with Milton Girls Softball. To determine if it was a viable option, we mapped all the locations of the girls who registered in 2013.  The map is attached (NOTE: if you zoom in real close you can see Dana waving from his window!).  You can see some areas of town where Milton Girls Softball is not strongly represented.  We decided to look at a few of these areas but the direct mail cost is not worth the investment.  We decided that we are better off focusing on the efforts above.

Google Keywords
We are experimenting with Google Keywords.  Under a free promotion, we have purchased keywords so when someone in the Boston DMA (essentially metro Boston) searches for keywords on google.com that we define, an ad for Milton Girls Softball will appear.  We only pay when someone clicks on it at a cost of approximately 2¢ per click.  

When someone clicks the ad, we are directing them to a special page we have created.  The page is targeting this search audience with the assumption that they do not know much about Milton Girls Softball and would like to learn more.  Here is a link to the page:
https://leagueathletics.com/Page.asp?n=18958&org=miltonsoftball.com

We are testing various keywords, but here are some in the mix right now:
  • milton girls softball
  • milton softball
  • milton ma softball
  • milton soccer (yes, we did)
  • milton lacrosse (yes, we did)
  • milton ma softball
  • milton travel softball
Overall, there are about 30 keywords/phrases we are testing.  If you have some suggestions, feel free to share.  It's easy to add words. We will provide some initial findings at the Feb meeting.

The keywords should be running in the next day or two.

Ideas?
If you have other ideas about how to promote registration and the program as a whole, please share yours thoughts.  We are always looking to make it better.
