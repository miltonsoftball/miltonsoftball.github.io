Indoor Pre Season Pitching Clinic at Amory

Posted by Gary Kelly on March 21, 2014

Our pitching clinic that was to start the other night (Wednesday) was canceled due to the Armory not having someone there as scheduled to open.  I was at the Armory that day to confirm for that night, and everything was a go, personnel from the Armory never showed to open that night.

I meet the 7 pm group there to sign them in, we all waited till 7:20 and then let them know we will add this night onto the back end, and apologized for the inconvenience.  We contacted the 8 pm attendees to let them know of the issue and rescheduled everyone to start next Wed.

I did revisit the Armory to find out the issue, they assured us it will not happen in the future, the next 5 weeks are reconfirmed, and I was able to obtain the personal phone number of the expected employee of the Armory that will be opening.

One positive would be to see if we can fill up all 20 slots. Going into Wednesday night, we had 15 participants signed up, with 5 spots open.
