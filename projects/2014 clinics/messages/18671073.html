Bill Vaughn on December 20, 2013:

We've finalized the initial phase of clinics for the coming season. Dirt Dawgs will be our partner for all around skill clinics. 8 weekly sessions on Sunday nights starting January 26 until March 23rd, skipping Super Bowl Sunday. There will be 2 sessions,from 5-8pm grades 3-5, and 6-8. Each will be 90 minutes. Cost will be $10 per girl per session (paid by MGS) Estimated 40-50 will sign up total, with estimated cost about 4k. Still to be finalized are two free pitching clinics at a indoor Milton location TBD, which will serve to narrow interested pitching candidates to an additional series of clinics that the families will pay for. Finally, we propose team and coaches clinics to be held possibly at TJO's, once the teams have been determined. Estimated cost for these are about $2k.
