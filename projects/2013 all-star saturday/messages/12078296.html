Shawn Ward on June 6, 2013:

My feedback:

positives:
- Overall day was fantastic 
- I talked to alot of parents/kids at the Freshman Skills -- they loved it: especially getting soaked under the hose
- sophomore game was a big hit -- kids (and their parents) loved being announced on the PA system
- I was in and out at juniors game, and not at the senior game (so no specific feedback their)
- coordination and volunteer effort was very strong - everyone came together and it never felt like a big scramble

negatives:
- whoever was responsible for the weather dropped the ball (much too hot)
- seemed like it wasn't well attended by non-all stars (weather could have played a role there) -- might want to consider creative ways to get others to attend
- the food/ bake sale didnt seem well positioned to attract alot of families that did attend

overall - -was a very successful day - and all I spoke to had a great time
