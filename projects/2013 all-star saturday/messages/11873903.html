Volunteer Schedule and Things We Need

Posted by Kevin Olivieri on May 27, 2013

Hi Board Members, 

I hope you had a good long weekend.  Today was definitely the prize of the weekend.

As you know All-Star Saturday is this Saturday, June 1st.  The extended weather forecast looks good.  Hopefully it wil stay that way.  We have three big "events" on Saturday:

  • Freshwomen Skills Challenge
  • Sophomore, Junior and Senior All-Star Games
  • Cookout

I have attached a document that will serve as the master volunteer schedule.  The second tab on the document is a working list of everything we need for the event.  The list will continue to grow I'm sure.  Kate Carroll is coordinating a bake sale that will align well with our cookout.  As we discussed at the board meeting, all proceeds from the bake sale and the cookout will go to the One Fund.

Please take a look at each tab (schedule and items we need) and let us know how you can help us make this Saturday a memorable event.  We can use as much time as you can sacrifice.  As Board Members, it is important we have a strong presence at the event in addition to encouraging others to volunteer their time.

A few of the big areas of need include making water balloons (we need about 130) and as well as someone who can help prep the fields before each game.  We have a new chalk liner we can use.  Plus we have many other needs.

Please review the document and reply to this thread with when you are available (and for what) and what you can bring.

We will continue to update the document throughout the week here on Basecamp.

Thank you for all your help.
