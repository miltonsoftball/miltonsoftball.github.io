Senior Draft Discussion

Posted by Karen Lewis on March 19, 2013

I came home and talked to Mitch about a fair way to draft players (not taking into consideration pitchers) and he has been in Fantasy Football for years. The way they do it is you get assigned a blind draw so if there are six teams 1-6 would be assigned. First round 1 goes first, 2 goes second, and so on. Second round 6 goes first, 5 goes second, and so on. Third round 1 goes first, 2 goes second, and so on. At first I was thinking this wasn't fair because 1 and 6 have an advantage and Mitch's reply was do you want the best and worst or do you want most of the middle players.

Something to think about.
