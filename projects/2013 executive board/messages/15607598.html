Agenda for September 16th Meeting

Posted by Kevin Olivieri on September 12, 2013

Hi there- 

Here is a rough draft of the structure and talking points of the meeting on Monday, 9/16.  Feel free to edit and comment as you see fit.  Gary - I am particularly wondering your reaction to the section about expectations.  If it's too strong, please let me know.  I want to be transparent but I also don't want to make you feel uncomfortable.  Let me know what you think.

---------

Immediately Prior to the Board Meeting
Kevin to talk to Rich and Kenny before the meeting (minutes before the meeting).

Expectations Not Met
Let me start by saying thank you for meeting again under such short notice.

As a leader, I have high expectations.  And when someone tells me they are going to do something, I expect it to be done.  There were more than several occurrences where I was told things would get done and they were not.  

Conversations were held and expectations were set on several occasions.  The results did not change.

It's not important what they were but I felt that they impacted the program and the girls in the program in negative ways.  It's not aligned with what I felt we were trying to build and I felt as a leader I had failed.


Miscalculation
Like I had expressed at the last meeting, I was comfortable with my decision to step down. After listening to you express your concerns and after Gary, Karen and I had a productive meeting last week, it was clear that I miscalculated the effort required for a smooth transition.


Commitment
Gary and I are staying on the Board. 

Gary has stepped down from the Executive Board but will remain on the board for at least 2 years.  I am extremely happy with this.  Gary is the face of Milton Girls Softball.  He has a strong love for the program and is wired into the community like no one else.  As a member of the Board, Gary will be focusing on X, Y and Z.

Karen has committed to 2 more years. Karen has been a rock for the last year and will continue to do so

I am also happy to report that Kenny J. and Rich F. have committed to the board for one more year.  

And I am staying on as president at least for another year.

Any questions?


Responsibilities
Talk about the structure of our board and importance of balancing workload and responsibilities.  

Hand out Committee list and descriptions
Karen to post large post-its

We are a working board and I need to do a better job at tapping into the experience and expertise that exists in this room. I want each of you to review the committees and select two that you will own and manage. Everyone needs to select at least two areas and one of those areas need to be as the lead.

Attending and volunteering at events is a given.  As a board member, we need your presence at the Parade, All-Star Game and Championship Games.

Spend time going over the groups.

Communicate dates for next two meetings

Adjorn
