Celebrate Milton!

Posted by Kevin Olivieri on September 14, 2013

I had an idea I wanted to run by the two of you.  What if we participated in Celebrate Milton? The idea is to increase our exposure to the rest of the town.  I think we have an opportunity to reach parents and girls who are unfamiliar with the program and/or would like to learn more about it.

We would have a table, set up a tent (maybe see if we can have a tent produced with our new logo), use the Milton Girls Softball Banner and have free chotskis (pink wristbands and magnets).  

The event is on Sunday, October 6th from 12-4.  I thinking we could staff it with 2 people at a time with 2 hours shifts each (12-2 and 2-4) and have a group for set up and breakdown.  I have hockey (not sure of time) but will try to come for some of it - maybe for set up and breakdown.

Here is the link for sponsorship levels:
http://www.celebratemilton.org/the-event/sponsors/business-sponsorship-registration/

I think the $60 or $130 would be fine.

What do you think?
