Gary Kelly on May 27, 2013:

HGSSL May 22 meeting (Mansfield, 8 pm).  I represented Milton for the submission of our 5 teams for the 2013 summer. 2 U14 teams, 2 U12, and one U10.  I submitted payment for 5 teams of $625, for league fee ($125 per team).  Submitted all required paper work, insurance binder, coaches info, with cell phones, (submitted printouts from website for Conroy, Tangney, Rundlett, Ward, and Carroll). 12 of 16 towns were present.

Towns made final declaration of number of summer teams they are submitting, along with paper work.  Once again, Milton had a strong representation submitting 5 teams.  Ex- Dedham who is in the hock, has no U14 teams, due to lack of players.

One rule change- 12 Runs after 5 instead of 15.

Next meeting is a Coaches meeting.  Brian Conroy and I were the only two coaches to make it last year.  They go over rules and expectations for coaches. June12th, location to be announced.  Town reps do not need to be present, coaches should be.

I will send out an email on wecare to the 5 coaches with the date and location to be announced.  Brian Conroy is the most seasoned Hock coach, with 2 coaches who will be new to summer ball and level of play.

Schedules are not ready, probably be out first week of June.  Season is June 17th to week of July 22nd.

Will look to submit summer budget to restrict funds collected for summer to be used for summer.  Current projections looks to be approx 65 players= $9750 total revenue.  Expenses approx- $7,000.  Will look to recommend summer travel equipment bags, catching equipment for some teams, helmets, coaching shirts, and batting instruction fees.
