Equipment Storage

Posted by Kevin Olivieri on June 30, 2013

As you know we have been discussing how to best store our equipment so we can keep it safe, clean and if possible, protect it from the elements.  After some follow-up conversations with Board Members, we investigated storage units at Extra Space Storage in Milton/Lower Mills.  We waited until a unit to our liking and needs opened up and this last weekend one did.

This will be a great place to house all equipment bags, extra equipment, banners, etc.  I understand this is a luxury for any league so if budgets get tight and we need to look at some cost saving measures, I think this will be the first to go.  

This will be a great place to have coaches pick up/drop off equipment as well as inventory all the bags in the off-season.

The monthly is just about $200 for a 10x10 unit.

Keypad access is 6500* (to get inside the building)
The unit is #82.
Combination for our unit is 0-30-35  (turn right once, turn left twice and turn right once)
