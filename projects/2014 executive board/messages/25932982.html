Hello Summer Coaches!

First of all, Karen, Bill and I want to thank you for volunteering to coach a summer travel team for Milton Girls Softball. You are important and visible representatives of Milton Girls Softball to the Milton community and beyond.  Thank you for your commitment and effort.
