Playoffs!

Posted by Kevin Olivieri on March 20, 2014

I would like to start this discussion thread by sharing this video link:
http://youtu.be/Qwq7BYOnDrM?t=40s

The playoff schedule has been added to the Master Calendar and the list of league events on the website.  The playoffs start on Sunday to avoid any issues with rainouts.  The first round of playoffs are when we have the most games so once we get through the first round, it should be manageable.

Here is what I proposed (but happy to change):

Sophomore - 7 teams
  • First round - 3 games on Sunday at 3pm
  • Semifinals - 2 games on Wed
  • Final - 1 game on Sat

Junior - 6 teams
  • First round - 2 games on Sunday at 5pm
  • Semifinals - 2 games on Tues
  • Final - 1 game on Sat

Senior - 6 teams
  • First round - 1 game on Sunday at 5pm
  • Semifinals - 2 games on Mon
  • Final - 1 game on Sat

Rainouts
If we rainout on Sunday...
  • Sophomore pushed to Monday (Semis on Wed)
  • Junior pushed to Tuesday (Semis on Thu)
  • Senior pushed to Tuesday (Semis on Thu)

If we have a rainout on Mon, Tue or Wed, the makeup day would be pushed to Thu.  The other days would not change.  For example, if we had a rainout Monday, the games on Tues and Wed stay as scheduled.  Monday's schedule moves to Thursday.

Here is a link to the master calendar: http://bit.ly/1ozLJ8s

Let me know what you think.
