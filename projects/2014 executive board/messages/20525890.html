Scheduling

Posted by Kevin Olivieri on January 15, 2014

I want to toss out an idea to the Executive Board.  I'd like you to give it some thought and maybe we can add it to our agenda.

Do you think that we should commit to each division playing on certain days at certain times?  For example, do we go public and state that Freshmen only play on Saturdays and Wednesdays.  Juniors only play on Saturdays from 9-2 and on Tue and Wed.  We wouldn't be able to control practice days but we could control game days.

 I know a lot of parents are planners and love to schedule.  I know we won't please everyone (i.e. Maggie has violin on Tuesdays) but I think if we advertise and promote our schedules, it may get the lacrosse and soccer kids to commit if they know the parameters.  

Something we can chat about at our next meeting.
