Online Shop

Posted by Kevin Olivieri on July 8, 2013

Hello Board!

I have been working on an online store on and off again over the past few years.  I have tried products from Cafepress, SquidLocker and others but was never really happy with the quality of the service and products they provided.  

Cafepress recently launched a new interface.  The tool provided me with a lot of room to customize the products offered as well as manage the look and feel of the website.  

The result is our official online store.  The store will not replace SuperFlash as our source for uniforms and equipment but it will provide members of the softball community with a place to purchase of wide variety of Milton Girls Softball products.  I created some custom apparel and if you think of other ideas, let me know.  I am happy to add more product.

Let me know what you think.  I plan to launch it publically later this week. Feedback welcome!
 
http://www.cafepress.com/miltonsoftball
