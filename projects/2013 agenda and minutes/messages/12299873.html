Gile Road (high school field)

Posted by Kevin Olivieri on June 10, 2013

Good news!

Much to our surprise, Gile Road is getting new dirt today (Monday).  We had hoped to be part of a discussion about the type of dirt and the irrigation system but it appears the town proceeded without us.

Gary confirmed with the town that the field will be playable for this evening.  Fingers crossed.

Gary Kelly on June 10, 2013:

Just communicated with Dave Peridos, field is just about done.  He said it is coming out great, and will be ready for tonight, barring any heavy rain.

As mentioned in prior thread, this was a last minute surprise, as the company doing the work, had a cancellation, and Peridos pulled the trigger.  The good news after discussion with Dave, is that he did choose to go with a better material, which has a heavier mixture of clay, which will hopefully hold down the dust issues.  We discussed the irrigation,  and he of course wanted to get the field done, as the opportunity arose, and as he mentioned the irrigation can be done at any time, and will be evaluated for a new system or potential of one, when they do Kelley field.  We are working on getting new irrigation at Kelley as well, and working on location and electrical of box.

It is going to be great to see the new material, with new regrading, and pitch.  This is expected to take care of plate issues, along with better draining.  We are expecting not to have the mini ponds behind short,third and 2nd for that matter.  Karen already took pictures of the work being done, it looks great.
