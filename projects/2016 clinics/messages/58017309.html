May/June Pitching Clinics

Posted by Kevin Olivieri on May 17, 2016

Hi all -

We kicked off our latest session of pitching clinics last night and the feedback was positive.

As a reminder, we scheduled Janelle for two sessions at 5pm and 6pm for 4 weeks each. We also hired Alexa D'Onofrio to run two additional sessions at the same timed/different field. Alexa is the pitching coach at ENC.

The clinics nearly sold out with only one slot open for Alexa's 6pm session. 

I observed Alexa's sessions for 10-15 minutes each. She is very personable, connected with the girls, and they were engaged. This was particularly the case with the first group where we had 6 girls who had never pitched before.

Good stuff.
