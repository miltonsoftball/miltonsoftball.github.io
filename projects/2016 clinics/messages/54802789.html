Clinics Update

Posted by Bill Vaughn on February 23, 2016

Good news all around. Last night was a success on both fronts. At Collicot, all three pitching clinics were filled to maximum. For those of you who would like to help out next two weeks, please respond to this thread with your availability. Thanks
