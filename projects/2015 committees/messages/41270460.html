Toilet Committee

Posted by Kevin Olivieri on April 27, 2015

Well, it's not really a committee. There was some discussion at a recent board meeting about bathrooms. I reached out and received the following quote. I am advocating this either way - I am simply sharing information. I would like to understand from other board members if this is something we pursue and if so, where and what coordination would need to happen with Park & Rec.

Here is the quote:

Hello Kevin, 

Thank you for allowing me to update you about NSC Restrooms.  Your inquiry about preventing vandalism is a good one.  We do offer a $10.00 damage waiver, but it does not cover all vandalism, it does cover standard damage, broken locks, hand sanitizer, etc...  Major vandalism is not covered by the policy.  You can put a lock on the outside to keep the inside from being vandalized.  Tip overs are also not covered by the damage waiver.  Feel free to call the office to discuss.

Northeast Sanitation Corporation is a provider of portable restrooms and services for Massachusetts, southern New Hampshire and Rhode Island. 

Please note our competitive pricing which is always firm through completion of each project.

* Our monthly rate is $98.00 per month, which includes one cleaning per week for four weeks for each portable restroom (28-day cycle, industry standard). Taxes are $0.44 per cycle. 

* There is a one time delivery fee of $10.00 per unit and insurance is optional at $10.00 per unit if elected. 

* NSC does NOT charge for pick-up. There are NO fuel surcharges and there are NO environmental fees. 

* A Winter Mix fee is charged from mid-November to mid-March, weather dependent, at $12.00 per month per unit. 

We pride ourselves for our reliable and consistent service, availability (24/7), communication (phone, e-mail or text) and fixed pricing.

Please call us to schedule delivery of clean units to your projects! 

Best regards,
