Calece Johnson on May 27, 2015:

5 applications were received and essays sent to Shawn yesterday. Committee is meeting tonight. I have the originals. 

Below is the process outlined for approving the recipients. Let me know when you need their contact info. 

1.    Tally votes and determine winners. The goal is the most amount of points.

a.    If there is a 1st place winner and a 2nd place winner then those are the two winners.

b.    If there is a tie for 1st place those two winners are the winners of the two scholarships.

c.    If there is a 1st place winner and there is a tie for 2nd place then the Executive Board of Directors reviews those two essays and votes on the winner.


Approval Process

1.    The Executive Board member presents the findings to the remaining two members.

2.    If there are any questions then the Review Committee has to meet to discuss. If there are no questions then the two scholarship recipients are contacted.

3.    The schools will be notified and the awards will be given to the schools for presentation at the schools award ceremony.
