Summer Budget

Posted by Kevin Olivieri on June 11, 2015

Hi all,

I spent time with our summer budget and reviewed it with Karen and Bill. As you know, we want the summer program to be self-sufficient. After including every possible expense we could think of, we essentially have about $900 in discretionary spending.

In evaluating the ideas and options presented (catcher's mitt, clinics, subsidize tourney fees, etc.), we decided that the best use of the $900 would be to have each summer team attend a Dirt Dawg hitting clinic.  Bill is working on the logistics but we plan to have this in place immediately so teams can schedule a DD session over the next week or two.

The summer budget is attached if you are curious how the budget breaks out.

If you have any questions or ideas that we should consider for next summer (i.e. raise the summer fee and include a tournament), we would love to hear them.

Thanks!
