Softball Summer Jam

Posted by Kevin Olivieri on August 4, 2015

Freshwomen Coach Sally Driscoll reached out to us a few weeks back about having a fun, laid back series of softball games for the K Program and Freshwomen Division. She wanted to provide the girls an opportunity to keep playing during the summer as well as prepare rising 3rd graders for the next level. 

As a trial, she is running 3 Softball Summer Jams. 90 minutes of softball each Thursday at Cunningham Park. She has recruited several Coaches as well as our own Gary Kelly!  She has the board's full support.

It will be a great experience for the girls and if it gains traction it may be something we can establish more formally next summer.

The info is on the website, twitter, facebook and an email was sent out to the K-2 parents/players.

2015 SUMMER JAM
SOFTBALL REGISTRATION
 


Welcome to the Registration Form for 2015 Summer Jam.

Summer Jam is a new summer program being offered by Milton Girls Softball. The goal of the program is to offer girls from the Kindergarten Program and the Freshwomen Division an opportunity to continue playing softball over the summer.

Current Milton Girls Softball coaches wil run skill sessions and mini games to help each group prepare for next Spring. Girls will be allowed to pitch with coach instruction. It will help Kindergartners experience simulated games.

The sessions will be fun, laid back and drop-ins are welcome.

Sign-up by going to http://assn.la/Reg/?r=4:98062


Eligibility: Any player who played in the Milton Girls Softball Spring Kindergarten or Freshwomen Program

Cost: Free

Dates:

  • Thursday, August 6th 5:30 -7pm

  • Thursday, August 13th 5:30-7pm

  • Thursday, August 20th 5:30-7pm

Location: Cunningham Park

What to Bring: Bring your glove, water and your spring jersy (optional)

 

Sign-up by going to http://assn.la/Reg/?r=4:98062  Look forward to seeing you Thursday!
