Helmet Initiative

Posted by Kevin Olivieri on March 14, 2013

As part of the surplus initiative, helmets for the Freshwomen and the Sophomore Divisions have been priced and approved  The Sophomore order has been placed.  The Freshwomen order will be placed after we receive a sample size from Superflash to confirm the size is appropriate for this age level.  The sample helmet should be available for us by 3/21.

We also ordered 15 helmets for the girls who paid for them during registration.

Finally, we ordered decals for the helmets.  The helmet style and the decal are attached for your enjoyment.
